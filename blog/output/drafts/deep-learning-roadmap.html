Getting Started with Deep Learning - Roadmap

First of all - the general overview of all the major topics, and the order in which to study them (made based on the overview of the Deep Learning from metacademy)

Now - the list of resources and projects, in order of learning:

1. Andrew Ng course

Andrew Ng Machine Learning course,
supplemented with his course on Deep learning , and Neural Networks explanation from Pedro Domingos ML course(for things that are unclear).

Learn the basics of math in the process, as needed from Khan Academy(Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability & Statistics.)

Projects:

  • Linear Regression
  • Logistic Regression
  • Feed Forward Neural Network
  • Backpropagation

2. Neural Networks for Machine Learning

Geoffrey Hinton's coursera course "Neural Networks for Machine Learning", supplemented with MIT Book on Deep Learning and UFLDL tutorial by Stanford (alternative link)

Projects:

  • Implement neural network with back-propagation to recognize hand-written digits from MNIST database:
    Digit Recognizer Kaggle contest

3. UFLD Tutorial

After understaning gradient descent and neural networks with back-propagation go through the UFLDL tutorial by Stanford

And read MIT Book on Deep Learning.

Projects:

  • Understand autoencoders, Restricted Boltzmann machines, create Deep Belief nets.
